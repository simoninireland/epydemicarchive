Welcome

This is the epydemic network archive, a repository of random (and other) networks for doing network science.

To download networks from the archive, you can either browse the entire collection or search the archive against tags or criteria such as size, degree distribution, and so on. You can also submit networks in a range of common file formats. Accessing the archive is unrestricted; to upload networks you need to register and then sign in.

However, this manual use of the archive is inconvenient for large-scale use. An easier way is to access the archive programmatically using its API. If your work makes use of the epydemic simulation framework then the archive can be used transparently as a source of networks for your simulations, and you can also submit networks directly from programs.
